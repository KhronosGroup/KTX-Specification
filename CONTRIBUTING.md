## Contributing

This project welcomes contributions and suggestions. All contributions require agreement to a Contributor License Agreement (CLA), confirming that you have the right to, and do, grant us the rights to use your contribution.
The full text of the CLA is available at: https://gist.github.com/KhronosWebservices/f7df35f3fb11010843ac

## Khronos Member Companies

Employees of Khronos member companies may be covered under an Open Source Corporate CLA signed by their organization. If your company has signed the corporate CLA, you should not be prompted to sign individually when submitting a pull request.

## Other Contributors

When you submit a pull request, CLA Assistant will prompt you if a signature is required. Clicking the "CLA not signed yet" button in GitHub will not automatically sign the CLA. You will be given the opportunity to review the agreement before signing.
